THE DOCUMENTARY

The overall subject of the documentary project was Fordham Theatre, as I am an executive board member of Fordham Experimental Theatre (FET), based at the Rose Hill Campus, while my documentary filming partner, JuJu Jaworski, is a directing major based at the Lincoln Center Campus. The subject of my cut, titled 'The Creative Spirits of FET' focuses on some of the people who are involved in the club, essentially those who make the club what it is today. During the filming process, I focused on interviewing people at Rose Hill involved with FET as a club while JuJu focused on interviewing people at Lincoln Center involved with theatre as a basis of study. Although lots of our filming was separate, we came together to film multiple interviews and collect b-roll footage across Fordham. Individually, we both interviewed around four or five people and edited our cuts of the documentary separately.
